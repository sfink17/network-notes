Types of Delays and their causes

  • Processing delay
    •   This is the time required read a packet's header and process that header.
  • Queueing Delay
    •   This is the time that a packet spends waiting, in the queue, to be transmitted.
  • Transmission Delay
    •   This is the amount of time that it takes to send all of the bits in a packet.
  • Propagation Delay
    •   This is the time required for the data to travel from one router to another.
      Propagation delay is related to the distance the information travles.
